Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026. This policy details our standard encryption protocols, KYC handling dossier, and cookie policies.

1. Data Handling & Security

At Blueskye City Home, we implement AES-256 military-grade encryption keys to safeguard client records. All databases containing financial dossiers, purchase ledgers, and transaction credentials are secured inside isolated networks with granular IAM rules. We do not sell or lease your contact dossier to any third-party marketing brokers.

2. KYC Verification Dossier

To maintain alignment with institutional real estate regulations, partners and buyers registering premium assets are required to upload Know Your Customer (KYC) documentation, which includes a government-issued photo ID (National Passport, Driver's License) and a physical proof of address.

Escrow Security: All uploaded KYC document files are processed via a secure TLS tunnel and stored inside encrypted off-site cloud vaults. Access is strictly audited and limited to authorized compliance directors during escrow and title perfection phases.

3. Cookies & Referral Tracking

We use functional cookies to manage active sessions and save favorite listings. For our elite affiliate program, we utilize secure tracking cookies with a 90-day lifespan to log referral links. When a visitor reaches our page via an affiliate tracking link, the cookie preserves the partner's unique ID to ensure commission override attribution remains locked.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your personal dossier stored in our system, correct any discrepancies, or request the deletion of your account. Deletion requests are processed within 14 business days, subject to regulatory transaction record retention rules.
