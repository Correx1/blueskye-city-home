1. Property Acquisition & Purchase Terms

Submitting a registration of interest through our multi-step purchase wizard does not constitute a binding sales agreement. It registers your interest inside our priority allocation queue.

All financial transfers are secured by our BlueSky Escrow Shield program. Property title and deed processing begin only after down payment clearance has been validated by our legal counsel. Defaulting on milestone installments beyond a 30-day grace period may result in transaction termination fees as outlined in your individual contract.

2. Elite Affiliate Network Regulations

By registering as an affiliate partner, you agree to represent the brand with integrity and avoid spamming marketing links.

Commission Milestones:

- Direct tier commissions (10%) are paid within 7 business days of escrow clearance.
- Override tier commissions (5%) are credited to the primary partner account immediately after secondary affiliate deals are cleared.
- Affiliate tracking links preserve click cookies for 90 days. Duplicate tracking redirects defer to the first referral cookie.

Clients booking site inspections (Physical or Virtual) must provide valid contact coordinates. Physical inspections are scheduled subject to safety regulations and property access clearances. Cancellations or schedule modifications should be submitted at least 24 hours prior to the booked tour slot. Virtual video tours are conducted over secure corporate conference channels.
